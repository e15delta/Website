Project 2: #TUXSAX

1. Inverted Trash Bin Lid

- A public trash bin was spotted with its lid placed upside-down.

-Despite the lid covering the cavity of the bin, trash has still been placed into it.

-A sticker writing out KOLORCANS.COM is present on the lid.

-On the same lid, a seemingly broken chain hangs into the dome-shaped interior.

2. Hollow Metal Gate

- A metal gate with no fencing in between its exterior, worn-out frame.

-The locking mechanism appears to still work and continues to be used to keep the gate in place.

-The gate is too wide to shut at a straight angle.

-Graffiti marks are apparent at the other side of the gate.

3. Blanked-Out Crosswalk Sign

- At one end of the crosswalk, a button with a faded-out sign is found.

-The message of the sign for the button is incomplete with only a piece of an arrow symbol remaining.

-Traces of what may have been a sticker remain over the missing portion of the sign.

-The button and sign are made of metal and are undamaged despite the tampering.

4. Edited Gas Station Sign

- A sign announcing the prices of gas and methods of pay are found on the sidewalk.

-The word “DEBIT” has been added onto the sign with the letter T placed outside of the actual sign and over the frame.

-The sign itself is dented in certain areas.

-Due to the dents, the text in some places is warped and distorted.
